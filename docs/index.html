ABOUT

This project was created for those that miss their days in Orbach Library. It was made to help people reminisce on the times spent with their friends and all the sensations that accompanied visiting the library. A lofi spotify playlist is provided on the left side of the webpage along with a series of ambient sounds such as other students talking, typing, a printer, and the sound of rain.
